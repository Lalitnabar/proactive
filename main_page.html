# Proactive Supervision Proactive supervision is a forward-looking approach aimed at strengthening the regulatory compliance and risk management frameworks of cooperative banks and regional rural banks (RRBs). This strategy involves regular, constructive engagement with bank leadership to ensure adherence to regulatory standards and prepare for risk-based supervision. Join us in fostering a secure and compliant banking environment for sustainable rural development.
